Our Legacy

A Legacy of Reliability in the Global Marine Industry

For more than four decades, INDO HELLAS has been a name synonymous with trust, efficiency, and excellence in ship supply services. Founded in 1996 in Chennai (Madras), we began with a mission to provide world-class ship chandling solutions to vessels operating across India.

What started as a local operation has grown into a globally connected marine service provider, supporting ships across major and minor Indian ports — and expanding internationally since 2018. Today, INDO HELLAS stands as one of the oldest and most reputed marine suppliers, trusted by shipowners, fleet managers, and maritime professionals worldwide.
